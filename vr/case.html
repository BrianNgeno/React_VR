Robbery and Violence

Case scenario

Facts of the case On 20th March 2005 at about 7pm, Sebastian Mun...ene, a teacher at Village Primary School walked out of young men. One of the young men asked him for Kshs 20 saying he had not eaten since morning. Sebastian gave him Ksh 100 as he was giving out the money, the other young man rushed towards him while armed with a knife and demanded for more money. They frisked Sebastian and robbed him of his mobile phone (Nokia x5), a Seiko watch and Ksh. 10,000. He identified PETER JUMA and MELVIN PETERS as accused person. The accused persons have denied taking the purse. After the incident, Sebastian Munene reported the incident to Village Police Station. Early the following morning as IP Thomas Joe was doing his normal patrol duties, he saw two young men who were sound asleep under a big tree. He woke them up, searched them and found ksh. 5000 and Seiko watch on them. The suspects were arrested and taken to the police station. They have been arraigned in court and they have pleaded not guilty. You are counsel for the accused, make a bail application for accused persons.
